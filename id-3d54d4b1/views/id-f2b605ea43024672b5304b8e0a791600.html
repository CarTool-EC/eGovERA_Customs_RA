OV-Preservation of cargo information [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The 'OV-Preservation of cargo information [Motivation] view extends the Organisational view and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging the preservation of cargo information. This view is represented by a Diagram Model-group ABB named 'Preservation of cargo information'. The 'Preservation of cargo information' is a Capability SBB that is responsible for preserving cargo information (DBC). This capability is supported by several Business Service ABBs, including 'Economic Operators Registry management', 'EU Customs Trader Portal', 'Tariff Registry management', 'Analysis Management', 'Customs declarations', 'Classification of goods', 'Preservation of cargo control', 'Intra-EU purchases and sales', 'Duties calculation and payment', and 'Duties guarantee management'. These services are all digital public services that contribute to the preservation of cargo information. The 'Preservation of cargo information' capability also interacts with several Business Object ABBs, which represent different types of information that are crucial for the preservation of cargo information. These include 'Imports information', 'Exports information', 'Special Procedures Information', 'Binding Tariffs Information', 'Economic value Information', 'Origin of goods Information', 'Guarantee Information', 'Economic Operators Information', 'Duties calculation Information', 'Combined nomenclature', and 'Classification of goods Information'. The relationships between the Capability SBB, Business Service ABBs, and Business Object ABBs in this view are crucial for the preservation of cargo information. The Business Service ABBs provide the necessary services to support the Capability SBB, while the Business Object ABBs provide the necessary information for these services. Together, these elements form a comprehensive framework for preserving cargo information.
