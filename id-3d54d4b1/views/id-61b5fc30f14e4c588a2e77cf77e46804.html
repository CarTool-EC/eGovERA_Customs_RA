OV-MS' Customs organization management [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The OV-MS' Customs organization management [Motivation] view extends the Organisational view and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging the capabilities and services of the MS' Customs organization management. This view is represented by a Diagram Model-group ABB, which encapsulates the various elements of the MS' Customs organization management. The MS' Customs organization management is a Capability SBB, which is responsible for managing the customs organization. This capability is supported by various Business Service ABBs, which provide digital public services related to customs operations. These services include Economic Operators Registry management, Determination of the economic value of goods declared for importation, Origin Registry management, EU Customs Trader Portal, Customs declarations, Tariff Registry management, Customs Decisions system, Procedure Automation System, Classification of goods, Tariff Suspensions, Goods flow analysis and simulations, Intra-EU purchases and sales, Duties calculation and payment, Notification of Arrival, Presentation Notification and Temporary Storage, and Duties guarantee management. These services are underpinned by various Business Object ABBs, which represent the information entities that are manipulated by the services. These objects include Origin of goods Information, Internal logistic Information, Binding Tariffs Information, Workforce information, Laboratories information, Guarantee Information, Classification of goods Information, Threads and fraudulent actions Information, Economic Operators Information, Economic value Information, Special Procedures Information, Temporary storage Information, Duties calculation Information, Free circulation requirement information, Combined nomenclature, Customs partners Information, Exports information, Imports information, and Economic Operators Registration and Identification number (EORI). The motivation behind this view is to provide a comprehensive understanding of the MS' Customs organization management's capabilities and services, and the information entities they manipulate. This understanding can be used to identify opportunities for improvement, streamline operations, and enhance the effectiveness and efficiency of the customs organization.
