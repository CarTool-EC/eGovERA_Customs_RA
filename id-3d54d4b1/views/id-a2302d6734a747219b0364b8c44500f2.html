OV-Customs DBCs, DPSs and Information [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The 'OV-Customs DBCs, DPSs and Information [Motivation] view extends the Organisational view and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging various elements related to customs digital business capabilities, digital public services, and information base. The view is divided into three main groupings: 'Customs Digital Public Services Catalogue', 'Customs Digital Business Capabilities Map', and 'Customs Information Base'. The 'Customs Digital Public Services Catalogue' grouping contains various business service ABBs such as 'Economic Operators Registry management', 'EU Customs Trader Portal', 'Analysis Management', and 'Centralised Clearance for Import' among others. These services represent different digital public services provided by the customs department. The 'Customs Digital Business Capabilities Map' grouping includes capability SBBs like 'Compliance requirements checks', 'Customs cooperation', 'Import procedure', and 'Guarantee' among others. These capabilities represent the digital business capabilities of the customs department. The 'Customs Information Base' grouping consists of business object ABBs such as 'Economic Operators Registration and Identification number (EORI)', 'Free circulation requirement information', 'Laboratories information', and 'Threads and fraudulent actions Information' among others. These objects represent the information base of the customs department. The view also includes several 'Diagram Model-connection ABB' elements, which are not described in the JSON but presumably represent the relationships and interactions between the different elements in the view. The motivation behind this view is to provide a comprehensive overview of the customs department's digital public services, digital business capabilities, and information base. This can help in understanding the department's operations, identifying areas for improvement, and developing strategies for digital transformation.
