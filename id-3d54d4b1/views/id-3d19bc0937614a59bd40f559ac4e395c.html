TVA-Customs Data Management Enablers [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The TVA-Customs Data Management Enablers [Motivation] view extends Technical view - application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging digital technologies to enhance the management and oversight of customs processes. This view is divided into two main elements: "Customs Data Management Enablers" and "Customs Digital Public Services". The "Customs Data Management Enablers" group comprises two application services: "Preservation of cargo information" and "Laboratories management". The "Preservation of cargo information" service refers to the secure and systematic management of data related to the movement and handling of goods during customs processes. This service involves the digital capture, storage, and retrieval of cargo-related information, such as shipping details, transport documents, customs declarations, and inspection reports. By leveraging digital technologies, including data management systems, document repositories, and secure data transmission protocols, this service ensures the integrity, traceability, and accessibility of critical data throughout the customs clearance process. The second service, "Laboratories management", refers to the implementation of digital tools and systems to efficiently manage and oversee customs laboratories' operations. This service involves the automation and optimization of laboratory processes, such as sample tracking, test management, result analysis, and quality assurance. By leveraging digital technologies like laboratory information management systems (LIMS), electronic data capture, and data analytics, this service enhances the accuracy, traceability, and efficiency of laboratory testing and analysis within the customs environment. The "Customs Digital Public Services" group includes two business services: "Preservation of cargo control" and "Laboratories registry management". These services represent the digital public services provided by the customs department to facilitate cargo control and manage laboratories registry. The exact nature and functionality of these services are not detailed in the provided JSON. In summary, the TVA-Customs Data Management Enablers [Motivation] view presents a framework that leverages digital technologies to enhance customs processes. It highlights the importance of data management and digital tools in ensuring efficient and effective customs operations.
