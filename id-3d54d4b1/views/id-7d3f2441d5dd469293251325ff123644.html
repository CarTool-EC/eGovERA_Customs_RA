TVA-Data Space Enablers [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The TVA-Data Space Enablers [Motivation] view extends Technical view - application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging various components and services that support the creation and management of a dataspace. This view is divided into three main elements: Dataspace Enablers, Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue, and Business Agnostic Dataspace Solutions. The Dataspace Enablers, a Grouping ABB, refers to components and services that support the creation and management of a dataspace. Within this grouping, there are two Application Service ABBs: Dataspace and Dataspace Connector. The Dataspace is the virtual space that enables the creation of a single market for data by integrating, transforming, and making available such data. The Dataspace Connector enables the easy and trustworthy procedure to share data and work collaboratively with other research institutions. The Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue is another Grouping ABB that includes two Business Service ABBs: Data Space and Data Space Participant. These services are part of the digital public service offerings related to the dataspace. The third element, Business Agnostic Dataspace Solutions, is a Grouping ABB that includes several Application Service SBBs. These are specific solutions for the dataspace, including the Eclipse Dataspace Connector, International Data Space Connector, FIWARE TRUE Connector, Sovity Dataspace Connector, Intel IONOS Orbiter Connector, and Fraunhofer AISEC Trusted Connector. Each of these services provides specific functionalities and capabilities within the dataspace. The motivation behind this view is to provide a comprehensive overview of the various components, services, and solutions that enable the creation, management, and utilization of a dataspace. This view helps in understanding the technical and business aspects of the dataspace and how they interact with each other. It also helps in identifying potential opportunities for improvement and innovation within the dataspace.
