OV-Compliance requirements checks [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The OV-Compliance requirements checks [Motivation] view extends the Organisational view and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging the compliance requirements checks in a digital public service context. This view is represented by a Diagram Model-group ABB named "Compliance requirements checks". Within this model, there is a Capability SBB also named "Compliance requirements checks". This capability is responsible for ensuring the compliance of various business services and business objects. The business services include "Origin Registry management", "Customs declarations", "Classification of goods", "Customs Decisions system", "Compliance verification management", "Duties calculation and payment", and "Duties guarantee management". These services represent different aspects of the digital public service that are subject to compliance checks. The business objects include "Economic value Information", "Special Procedures Information", "Binding Tariffs Information", "Guarantee Information", "Classification of goods Information", "Origin of goods Information", "Economic Operators Information", "Duties calculation Information", "Exports information", "Imports information", and "Combined nomenclature". These objects represent different types of information that are used in the digital public service and are subject to compliance checks. The motivation behind this view is to ensure that all aspects of the digital public service, from the services provided to the information used, are compliant with the necessary regulations and standards. This is achieved through the "Compliance requirements checks" capability, which checks the compliance of the business services and business objects. This ensures the integrity and reliability of the digital public service, and helps to maintain trust and confidence in the service.
