dct:descriptionThese articles from the Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2016/341 pertain to the rules and procedures for the operation of the power system. Article 12 discusses the obligation of transmission system operators to ensure system security, while Article 13 outlines the requirements for system operation guidelines.

skos:exampleAn example of applying these regulations could be a transmission system operator in the EU ensuring that their operations are secure and efficient, following the guidelines set out in Article 13. They would need to regularly monitor and assess the security of their system, and take any necessary measures to maintain operational security and frequency quality.

skos:noteArticle 12 requires transmission system operators to ensure system security, operational security, and the optimal use of the transmission infrastructure. They must also ensure that the system operation guidelines are followed. Article 13 states that system operation guidelines should be established to maintain operational security, frequency quality, and the efficient use of infrastructure and resources.
