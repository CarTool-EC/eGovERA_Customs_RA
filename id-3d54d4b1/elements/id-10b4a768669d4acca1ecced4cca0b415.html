National Customs Specific Taxonomy ()
Definition: Refers to a specialized classification framework developed and implemented by a specific country's customs authority. This taxonomy is tailored to categorize and organize customs-related data, regulations, and procedures unique to that nation's trade and customs environment. It provides a standardized structure that aids in consistent data classification, harmonization of customs processes, and effective management of customs-related information within the country's customs ecosystem. This taxonomy enables efficient customs operations, enhances trade facilitation, and supports accurate reporting and analysis while catering to the specific requirements and nuances of the national customs context.
