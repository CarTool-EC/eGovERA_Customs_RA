dct:descriptionArticle 3 of the Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2016/341 of 17 December 2015 pertains to the 'Opening of the tendering procedure'. It outlines the process and requirements for opening a tendering procedure for the buying-in of skimmed milk powder at a fixed price.

skos:exampleAn example of this regulation in action would be if there was a surplus of skimmed milk powder in the European Union. The European Commission would then initiate a tendering procedure to buy-in this surplus at a fixed price. The procedure would be opened by publishing a regulation in the Official Journal of the European Union, detailing the specifics of the tender, such as the quantity of skimmed milk powder to be bought, the fixed price, and the deadline for submission of tenders.

skos:noteThe regulation stipulates that the European Commission shall open the tendering procedure for the buying-in of skimmed milk powder at a fixed price. The procedure shall be opened by means of a regulation which shall be published in the Official Journal of the European Union. The regulation also provides details on the information to be included in the notice of the tendering procedure.
