MRN follow-up – Movement Reference Number ()
Description: The Movement Reference Number (MRN) is a unique identifier assigned to customs declarations when goods are moved between different customs offices or territories within the EU. The MRN follow-up system allows customs authorities and stakeholders to track the movement and status of goods in real-time. It provides visibility and transparency throughout the entire customs clearance process, allowing for efficient monitoring, risk assessment, and coordination between customs offices and economic operators. By leveraging the MRN follow-up system, the TCU aims to streamline customs operations, enhance trade facilitation, and ensure the secure and timely movement of goods across borders within the European Union.
