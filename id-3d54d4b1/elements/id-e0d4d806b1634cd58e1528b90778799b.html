TARIC (Integrated Tariff of the European Union) ()
Description: TARIC is an integrated tariff database that contains information on customs tariffs, import and export formalities, and applicable measures such as quotas, duties, and restrictions. It provides details on the classification of goods and determines the customs duties and trade policies associated with specific products.
