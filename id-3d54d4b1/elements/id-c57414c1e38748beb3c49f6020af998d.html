dct:descriptionArticle 6 of the Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2016/341 pertains to the 'Entry into the temporary storage facility'. It outlines the conditions and procedures for the entry of non-Union goods into the temporary storage facility. It specifies the responsibilities of the operator of the temporary storage facility and the conditions under which the goods may be moved within the facility.

skos:exampleFor instance, if a shipment of non-Union goods arrives at a port in the EU, the operator of the temporary storage facility at the port must ensure that the goods are presented to customs without delay. If the goods need to be moved within the facility for any reason, such as for inspection or for storage, this must be done under the supervision of the operator and in accordance with the instructions of the customs authorities.

skos:noteThe regulation stipulates that the operator of the temporary storage facility is responsible for ensuring that non-Union goods are presented to customs without delay. It also states that the goods may be moved within the facility only under the supervision of the operator and in accordance with the instructions of the customs authorities.
