Customs Trade Facilitation and Collaboration Enablers ()
Definition: Customs Trade Facilitation and Collaboration Enablers ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of digital platforms and systems designed to streamline and enhance the efficiency of customs operations, promote collaboration and information exchange between customs authorities, traders, and other stakeholders, and facilitate cross-border trade. The elements within this grouping include the Trade Facilitation Portal, Cooperation between Customs laboratories, Cooperation between Customs and Postal Operators, and MS' Customs organization management. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The elements within the Customs Trade Facilitation and Collaboration Enablers ABB grouping are digital solutions that leverage technologies such as electronic data interchange (EDI), data analytics, and laboratory information management systems (LIMS) to automate customs procedures, enhance transparency and security, improve data accuracy and consistency, and promote efficiency in cross-border trade. These digital platforms and systems provide functionalities such as electronic submission of trade documents, real-time tracking of shipments, automated customs processes, online payment options, and access to relevant trade regulations and guidelines. They also facilitate seamless collaboration, information sharing, and data exchange between customs authorities, traders, postal operators, and customs laboratories. Example: Trade Facilitation Portal An online platform that provides a centralized and user-friendly interface for traders, customs authorities, and other stakeholders to access and exchange trade-related information and services. Cooperation between Customs laboratories The implementation of digital platforms and systems to facilitate seamless collaboration, information sharing, and data exchange between customs laboratories. Cooperation between Customs and Postal Operators The implementation of digital platforms and systems to facilitate seamless collaboration, information exchange, and data sharing between customs authorities and postal operators. MS' Customs organization management The implementation of digital platforms and systems to effectively manage and oversee the operations of customs organizations within a specific country or region.
