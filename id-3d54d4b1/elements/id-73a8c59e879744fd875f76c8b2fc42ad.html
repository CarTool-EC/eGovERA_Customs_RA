Customs Data Format, Representation and Syntax Solutions ()
Definition: Customs Data Format, Representation and Syntax Solutions ABB is a grouping that covers the specific formats and syntax solutions used for customs declarations. This includes the structures and fields used to capture information about import or export shipments, such as details about goods, their classification, valuation, origin, and other customs-related information. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Customs Data Format, Representation and Syntax Solutions ABB is a comprehensive catalogue of elements that are used in the customs declaration process. These elements are designed to standardize the way information is captured and represented, ensuring that all necessary details are accurately and consistently recorded. This includes information about the goods being imported or exported, their classification according to customs regulations, their valuation, their origin, and any other information that may be relevant to the customs process. The elements in this catalogue are used in various customs declaration formats, including the EDIFACT CUSDEC format.
