Customs Data Exchange Solutions ()
Definition: Customs Data Exchange Solutions ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of solutions designed to facilitate the exchange of customs data between different entities. The main objective of these solutions is to streamline the processes of data management and make all relevant information readily available. The European Commission has designed a centralised system for this purpose, known as the INF SP System. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: Customs Data Exchange Solutions ABB includes the 'Central system for the standardised exchange of information', also known as the INF SP System. This system is designed to standardise the exchange of information and streamline the processes of data management. It aims to make all INF data available in a centralised manner, thereby improving efficiency and reducing the potential for errors. The system is designed by the European Commission and is part of their efforts to improve customs procedures across the EU. Example: An example of the application of the Customs Data Exchange Solutions ABB is the use of the INF SP System by customs authorities across the EU. This system allows for the standardised exchange of information, making it easier for these authorities to access and manage the data they need. This can help to speed up customs procedures and improve the accuracy of the data being used.
