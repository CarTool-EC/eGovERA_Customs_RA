Reference Data & Customs Offices List ()
Description: The ‘Reference Data & Customs Office List’ pages provide information on the Reference Data (RD) and the Customs Office List (COL) used by the national customs applications. The Reference Data part is maintained by the Commission. The Customs Office List published on this site is based on the data provided and maintained by the national customs administrations. Those data are provided for information only and is binding neither for the Commission nor for the National Administrations concerned.
