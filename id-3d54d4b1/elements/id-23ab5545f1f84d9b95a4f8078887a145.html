AEO - Authorised Economic Operator ()
Description: AEO stands for Authorized Economic Operator. It is a status granted to businesses by customs administrations in the European Union (EU) and many other countries worldwide. The AEO program aims to enhance international supply chain security and facilitate trade by recognizing businesses that meet certain criteria and demonstrate compliance with customs regulations and security standards.
