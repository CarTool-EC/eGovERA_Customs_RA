Customs Dataset Catalogue ()
Definition: Customs Dataset Catalogue ABB is a Grouping that covers a wide range of metadata and indices related to customs operations and procedures. It includes elements such as EORI metadata, customs statistical metadata on goods imported and exported, applications and requests metadata, laboratory reports index, tariffs metadata, risk metadata, economic operators metadata, origins metadata, cargo metadata, declarations metadata, intra-EU purchases and sales metadata, goods taxonomy metadata, notifications metadata, procedures metadata, goods metadata, and laboratories testing metadata. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Customs Dataset Catalogue ABB is a comprehensive collection of metadata and indices that are crucial for customs operations and procedures. These elements cover a wide range of areas including but not limited to, information about economic operators (EORI metadata), statistical data on goods imported and exported, metadata related to customs applications and requests, indices of laboratory reports, metadata on customs tariffs and risks, information on the origins of goods, cargo details, customs declarations, intra-EU purchases and sales, taxonomy of goods, customs notifications and procedures, and metadata on goods and laboratory testing. This catalogue is a valuable resource for customs authorities, traders, and other stakeholders in the customs and trade sector. Example: For instance, the 'EORI metadata' element can be used by customs authorities to verify the identity of economic operators. The 'Customs statistical metadata on goods imported' and 'Customs statistical metadata on goods exported' elements provide valuable data for trade analysis and policy-making. The 'Applications and Requests metadata' element can be used to track and manage customs applications and requests. The 'Laboratory reports index' and 'Laboratories testing metadata' elements provide information on laboratory tests conducted on goods for customs purposes.
