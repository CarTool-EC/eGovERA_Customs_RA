CDS - Customs Decisions System ()
Description: The CDS is based on an IT architecture with both national and EU common components. 1. The central CDS is to be used for all applications and decisions which may have an impact in more than one Member State, and for any subsequent event which may affect the original application or decision (annulment, suspension, revocation or amendment); 2. Some Member States will use the CDS also to manage their national customs decisions (i.e. which have only a national impact), whilst other Member States will deploy their national CDS.
