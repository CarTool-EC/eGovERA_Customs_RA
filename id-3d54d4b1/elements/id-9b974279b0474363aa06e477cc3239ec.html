Customs Process Streamlining and Efficiency Enablers ()
Definition: Customs Process Streamlining and Efficiency Enablers ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of digital tools and technologies designed to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of customs operations. These include systems for expeditions controls, people and skill allocation and distribution, and guarantee management. These digital solutions provide customs authorities with real-time visibility and control over various aspects of the customs process, from the movement and condition of goods to the allocation of human resources and the management of customs guarantees. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The elements within the Customs Process Streamlining and Efficiency Enablers ABB are designed to automate and streamline various aspects of the customs process. Expeditions controls involve the use of digital systems to monitor, track, and control goods throughout the transportation and logistics process. People and skill allocation and distribution refers to digital workforce management systems that optimize personnel deployment and task assignment. Guarantee management involves the electronic administration of customs guarantees to ensure compliance with customs requirements. These digital solutions enhance supply chain security, improve workforce planning, reduce administrative burdens, and facilitate trade facilitation. Example: Examples of application for the elements within the Customs Process Streamlining and Efficiency Enablers ABB include the use of Electronic Data Interchange (EDI), Global Positioning System (GPS), and Internet of Things (IoT) devices for expeditions controls; digital workforce management systems for people and skill allocation and distribution; and digital platforms for the management and administration of customs guarantees.
