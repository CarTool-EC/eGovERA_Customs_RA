EBTI - European Binding Tariff Information ()
Description: EBTI - European Binding Tariff Information offers economic operators a legally binding decision on the classification of goods for customs purposes. By submitting a request with detailed information about the goods, importers and exporters can receive a ruling on the correct tariff classification from the customs authorities. This information is essential for determining the appropriate customs duties, taxes, and regulations applicable to specific goods. The EBTI service enhances trade predictability and facilitates compliance by providing certainty on the customs classification of goods, promoting efficient customs procedures and trade facilitation within the EU.
