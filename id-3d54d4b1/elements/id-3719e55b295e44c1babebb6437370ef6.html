EORI - validation open interface ()
Description: A digital solution provided by the Tax and Customs Union (TCU) of the European Union. The open interface allows external systems and stakeholders to validate the authenticity and correctness of EORI numbers in real-time. This service ensures that customs declarations, trade documents, and other transactions involving economic operators are accurate and legitimate. By offering this open interface, the TCU aims to promote secure and efficient cross-border trade by enabling seamless data validation and verification, enhancing customs control, and facilitating smooth interactions between economic operators and customs authorities.
