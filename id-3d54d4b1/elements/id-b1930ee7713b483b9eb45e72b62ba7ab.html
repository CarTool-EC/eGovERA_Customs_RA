Customs Data Management Enablers ()
Definition: Customs Data Management Enablers ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of digital solutions and services designed to enhance the management, preservation, and accessibility of data related to customs processes. These solutions leverage advanced digital technologies to ensure the integrity, traceability, and efficiency of customs operations, from the handling and storage of cargo information to the management of customs laboratories. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Customs Data Management Enablers ABB includes two key elements: Preservation of cargo information and Laboratories management. The former refers to the digital capture, storage, and retrieval of cargo-related information, such as shipping details, transport documents, customs declarations, and inspection reports. This service ensures the integrity, traceability, and accessibility of critical data throughout the customs clearance process. The latter refers to the implementation of digital tools and systems to manage and oversee customs laboratories' operations, enhancing the accuracy, traceability, and efficiency of laboratory testing and analysis within the customs environment. Example: Examples of application for the Customs Data Management Enablers ABB include a digital platform for the capture and storage of cargo information, which can be accessed by customs officers for verification and compliance checks. Another example is a laboratory information management system (LIMS) that automates and optimizes laboratory processes, such as sample tracking, test management, result analysis, and quality assurance, within the customs environment.
