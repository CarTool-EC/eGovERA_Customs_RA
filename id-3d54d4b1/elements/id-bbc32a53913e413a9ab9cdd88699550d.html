Currency Code List ()
Description: The Currency authority table is a controlled vocabulary that lists concepts associated with currencies and currency subunits. The concepts included are correlated with the ISO 4217 international standard. The Currency authority table is updated based on the stakeholders’ needs. Contributions to it are accepted following a review made by EU Vocabularies team of the Publications Office of the EU. The Currency authority table is maintained by the Publications Office of the European Union on the EU Vocabularies website.
