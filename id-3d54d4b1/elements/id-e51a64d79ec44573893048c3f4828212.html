TARIC - EU Customs Tariff ()
Description: TARIC - EU Customs Tariff is an extensive database that contains detailed information on the customs tariffs and regulations applicable to various goods imported into or exported from the EU. The TARIC system classifies goods using a combination of the Harmonized System (HS) codes and additional EU-specific codes. Economic operators can use TARIC to determine the customs duties, taxes, and other regulatory measures that apply to specific products when trading with EU member states. The TARIC database is regularly updated to reflect changes in customs regulations, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information for customs declarations and trade facilitation within the European Union.
