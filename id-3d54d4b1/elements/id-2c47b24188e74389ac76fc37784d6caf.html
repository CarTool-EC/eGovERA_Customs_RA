Combined Nomenclature ()
Description: The Combined Nomenclature (CN) is a tool for classifying goods, set up to meet the requirements both of the Common Customs TariffEN••• and of the EU's external trade statistics. The CN is also used in intra-EU trade statistics. It is a further development (with special EU-specific subdivisions) of the World Customs Organization'sHarmonized System nomenclature. This is a systematic list of commodities applied by most trading nations (and also used for international trade negotiations).
