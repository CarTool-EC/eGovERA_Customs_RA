Customs Dataset Solutions ()
Definition: Customs Dataset Solutions ABB is a Grouping that covers the collection, organization, and management of data related to customs operations. It includes elements such as EORI metadata and Economic operators metadata, which are crucial for the smooth functioning of customs procedures and operations. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Customs Dataset Solutions ABB is a comprehensive collection of data solutions designed to streamline and enhance customs operations. It includes elements like 'EORI metadata', which refers to data related to the Economic Operators Registration and Identification number, a unique ID assigned to businesses that interact with customs authorities. Another element is 'Economic operators metadata', which pertains to data about economic operators involved in customs operations. These elements are crucial for ensuring efficient customs procedures, facilitating international trade, and ensuring compliance with customs regulations. Example: For instance, the EORI metadata could be used to quickly identify and verify the details of a business involved in importing or exporting goods. The Economic operators metadata, on the other hand, could provide valuable insights into the operations of economic operators, helping customs authorities to monitor and regulate trade activities more effectively.
