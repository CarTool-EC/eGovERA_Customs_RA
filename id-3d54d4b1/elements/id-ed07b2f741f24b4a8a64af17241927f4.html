ICS (Import Control System) ()
Description: The ICS is an EU system designed to enhance customs control over goods entering the EU. It involves the submission of advanced electronic entry summary declarations by carriers or their representatives before the goods arrive in the EU. The ICS helps customs authorities identify high-risk consignments and streamline import procedures.
