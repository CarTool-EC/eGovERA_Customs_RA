skos:exampleFor instance, a company importing goods into the EU would need to comply with the procedures outlined in Regulation (EU) No 952/2013. This could involve declaring the goods to customs, providing necessary documentation, and ensuring that any duties or taxes are paid. The company may also benefit from simplified procedures if they are recognized as an Authorized Economic Operator (AEO).
eira:concepteira:SolutionBuildingBlock
skos:noteThe Union Customs Code (UCC) is a key element of the ongoing actions to modernize EU customs. It provides a comprehensive framework for customs rules and procedures in the EU customs territory adapted to modern trade realities and modern communication tools. The UCC aims to complete the shift by customs to a paperless and fully electronic environment, and reinforces swifter customs procedures for compliant and trustworthy economic operators (AEO concept).
